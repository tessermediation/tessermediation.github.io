Rates & Policies

I book one mediation per day because family mediation generally takes most or all of the day.  I'm happy to start as early as 8:00 a.m. or as late as 1:00 p.m. for relatively simple issues.

My rate is $360 per hour (which is $180 per party).  I'm presently on the Cobb, Gwinnett and Fulton lists (although, as a registered mediator, I am licensed to mediate family cases throughout the state).

Cancellation Policy

I ask that cancellation of mediation or conflicts that may create a cancellation be communicated to all concerned as soon as possible.

Cancellations within 48 hours of the mediation are subject to a 2 hour ($720) fee to the canceling party and counsel.
